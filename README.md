Gyptix: Your Private AI Companion

Gyptix is an open-source application designed as a "GPT toy" for macOS and iOS platforms.

Works on 📱 iPhone & iPad as well as on 💻 Apple Silicone Macs

It allows users to experiment with local "on device" AI.

Gyptix offers an intuitive and interactive interface for experimenting with GPT-based language models.

Ask questions, get instant AI-powered answers — completely offline.

Why Choose Gyptix?
* Absolutely FREE – No hidden costs or subscriptions.
* 100% Private – No personal data collection, ever.
* Your Data, Your Control – Nothing is stored or shared.
* No Internet Required – Works entirely on your mobile device.
* Local AI Engine – Runs independently without network access.
* True Offline Mode – Functions even in airplane mode.

Enjoy seamless, secure, and private AI assistance—anytime, anywhere.
